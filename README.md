# McDonald's South America Sales Dashboard 2022

## 📋 Project Overview

This is an **interactive Excel Dashboard** that analyzes McDonald's sales performance across **South America** in 2022.

The dashboard provides clear insights into **Sales**, **Profit**, **Customer Volume**, monthly trends, country-wise performance, and **Customer Satisfaction** metrics — all built using **Microsoft Excel** (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers, and Dashboard layout).

**Key Highlights:**
- Total Sales: **$2,544 Million USD**
- Total Profit: **$890 Million USD**
- Total Customers: **87 Million**
- Strongest performing countries: Argentina, Brazil, and Ecuador
- Highest customer satisfaction areas: Hygiene (93%) and Availability (95%)

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Track sales and profit performance against targets for 2022
- Compare monthly sales trends between 2021 and 2022
- Identify top-performing countries in South America
- Evaluate customer satisfaction across key service attributes
- Support data-driven decision making for regional management
